week 5: 3D Design & Printing

assignment: model and 3D print something

different infill patterns

i decided to take this week to figure out wheat infill pattern i want for the 3d printed fabric portion of my dress. for this initial print, i wasn't able to use the more flexible 3d printer filament, so my tests were only done with PLA filament. some of my initial prints were too thin which made it hard for the printer to completely print the pattern successfully. however, after changing the thickness i got a couple successful prints. the infill patterns i tried were rectilinear, stars, and hilbert curve.
out of these, i like the stars the best. i really liked the hilbert curve pattern, but the infill wasn't fully connected so it wouldn't have made good fabric.
